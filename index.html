charity: water
In a sunlit courtyard surrounded by thatched fences, Amina stands tall, her smile shining as brightly as the water she uses to wash dishes. With each soapy swirl, she is not just cleaning plates — she is nurturing her family and community.

Before the new well, washing dishes meant using dirty water, risking illness, and spending hours collecting every drop. Today, thanks to generous donors, Amina fills her green basin with clean, accessible water, and carries out this simple but essential chore with pride and joy.

Her laughter drifts across the courtyard, reminding us that the gift of clean water transforms even the most ordinary tasks into moments of health, hope, and dignity.
